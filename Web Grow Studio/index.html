Description:

Located on Punta Cana's famous Bavaro Beach along a seemingly endless path of stunning white sand and vivid, turquoise waters, sits the all-inclusive Occidental Punta Cana. Enjoy the tranquil atmosphere and Spanish-style architecture while being surrounded by tropical gardens and breathtaking views.

The resort features over 860 colonial-style rooms that are ideal for either families or couples, up to 11 restaurants, 10 bars, four swimming pools including one with a spectacular waterfall, and live music shows and Latin dance classes at the Mangu Club. Enjoy all the water sport action available under the tropical sun, like windsurfing, water polo, scuba diving, plus other sports activities within the hotel's facilities.
